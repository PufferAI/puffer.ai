PufferLib is a fast and sane reinforcement learning library that can train tiny, super-human models in seconds. The included learning algorithm, hyperparameter tuning, and simulation methods are the product of our own research. All our tools are free and open source, but you can purchase priority service to get our eyes on your problem from $10k/month. We also offer fixed-deliverables terms for larger problems.
